A Guide to Appliance Energy Ratings in Australia

Australian households are encouraged to use energy-efficient appliances to lower costs and reduce carbon emissions. The Energy Rating Label helps consumers compare the efficiency of different products before purchasing.

  • Look for appliances with more stars on the Energy Rating Label.
  • Consider the total energy consumption (kWh/year) shown on the label.
  • Efficient appliances can save money and help the environment.

Learn more at the Energy Rating website.
